    Base64 Encoder / Decoder

    Encode Base64 #

    # From text
$ zuk "Hello world!" to base64

# From pipe
$ zuk base64 < data.txt

    Decode Base64 #

    $ zuk SGVsbG8gd29ybGQh
    Too short text may not be recognized as a Base64 input.
