# Hex

Hexadecimal  Encoder / Decoder

## Examples

### Encode

<pre class="example">
<code>"Hello world!" to hex</code>
</pre>

### Decode

<pre class="example">
<code>48656c6c6f20776f726c6421</code>
</pre>

{{< hint warning >}}
Too short text may not be recognized as a hexadecimal input.
{{< /hint >}}
